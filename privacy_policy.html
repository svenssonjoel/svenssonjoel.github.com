Privacy Policy

My webpages use some scripts from third party vendors. For example, I use Google analytics to get an idea what kind of content is appreciated.

These scripts from third party vendors may store and access cookies on your computer and may use that information for ad personalization.

The following third party services are used on my webpages:
  • Google analytics.
  • Google custom search.
  • Google AdSense ads.
  • Youtube links.

The youtube links themselves do not store or read cookies, but following them to youtube will surely mean that some information of that nature is exchanged.

The services mentioned above are all provided by google and you can set your preferences in relation to ad personalization by visiting this page, ad settings.

This site shows information from google on how the information that is obtained from sites, such as mine, is used.

If you want to read some general information about cookies, check out this link .

You are in control, via your web browser's setting, how cookies are managed, stored and accepted or rejected. You can also at any time whipe all or specific cookies from your machine.
