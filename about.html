About

My name is Joel Svensson and I work for the swedish research institutes (RISE) within the area of dependable systems. This is however my personal webpage where I share my interests in a broader sense.

During 2019 I started making youtube videos (I know they are goofy but I hope improve as time goes by). I enjoy learning new things, and I want my videos to convey or illustrate that it is possible to "do things" even if you are not an expert in the area. Currently I am having a lot of fun with electronics and making my own PCBs using KiCad , something I thought would be completely impossible for someone who is not an electrical engineer. I believe that if we all help each other out and share our experiences and learning efforts, we can all do fun and interesting stuff in areas we previously thought to be out of reach. Most hardware design and code that I develop in the videos are released freely as open source so please clone, modify and share .
