Introduction

LispBM is a lisp-like language implemented by a non-lisper. The inspiration for LispBM came from watching the SICP course on youtube and a tiny amount of experimenting with EMACS lisp. I strongly recommend that you watch the SICP course, it is a lot of fun!

LispBM does not try to implement any particular Lisp or Scheme standard but may be more scheme-like in nature.
